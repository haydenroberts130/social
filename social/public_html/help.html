Help Center

Social Features

Your Profile

Your profile page is where all of your photos are located at. You can see each of your posts, the likes, and comments all in one place.

In addition, you can manage all the aspects of your posts, such as editing the post caption, deleting the post, and even deleting unwanted comments from other users.

Sharing Photos

To share your photos and have them show on your profile, click the '+' button located on the right of the top bar. There, you will be directed to the post page, where you can select a photo and add a caption before posting. Then, anyone can see, like, and comment on your post.

Viewing Photos

By default, you will be able to view photos of people you are following in your feed. To view a specific person's post, search them up in the search bar to reach thier profile. By following them, you will be able to see their posts in the future in your feed.

Account Management

Signing up and Getting Started

To sign up, navigate to the home page, where upon entering the desired username and password, a new account can be created. It is advised to use proper password safety when creating your account.
